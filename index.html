What is Satoshi•Pie?

Satoshi•Pie is a blockchain traded fund managed by Satoshi•Fund. It was created on 9 of May 2016. Initial capital is $50k was provided by asset managers: 21hipster, cybermonetarist and creator. Satoshi•Pie grew in a month to $0.5M under management. The base price increased by 40% over the past month in US dollars and by 12% in bitcoins. Initially it was for friends only. But due to increasing demand we begin to slowly rolling out it to a broader public.
